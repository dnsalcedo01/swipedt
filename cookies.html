Cookie Policy

We only use what we absolutely need. No tracking, no analytics, no third-party ad networks.

Essential Cookies Only

Our platform uses highly restricted, strictly essential browser technologies. We utilize standard session cookies solely to keep you securely logged into your account. Without this, the application cannot function securely.

Local Storage for Preferences

Instead of intrusive cookies, we use your browser's local storage to remember your UI preferences, specifically whether you prefer 'dark' or 'light' mode. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers.

No Third-Party Trackers

As stated in our Privacy Policy, we do not run advertisements. Therefore, we do not utilize third-party cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-site fingerprinting scripts. Your digital footprint remains yours alone.
