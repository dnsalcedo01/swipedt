Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our ultimate priority. Discover how we protect your data, why we reject advertisements, and how we keep you secure.

1. No Ads, Ever.

We fundamentally believe that advertisements not only disrupt your user experience but inherently compromise your privacy and personal data. Because we do not engage in such practices, you will never see third-party ads on our platform, nor will we ever sell your personal information to data brokers or advertising networks.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the bare minimum required to provide our service to you. This includes information such as your full name, username, email address, birthday, gender, password (securely hashed), and the content you voluntarily post (such as texts, images, and interactions). We temporarily process and log anonymized IP addresses purely for security purposes, such as rate-limiting to prevent abuse and brute-force attacks. We do not scrape your device, track your off-site browsing, or harvest unnecessary background data.

3. Security

We employ modern encryption methodologies to ensure your data is secure at rest and in transit. While no system is perfectly secure, our architecture is designed from the ground up to minimize attack surfaces and protect your identity.
