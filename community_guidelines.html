Community Guidelines

Help us keep Swipedt a safe, fun, and engaging place for everyone. Here are the core principles of our community.

1. Respect Each Other

Treat everyone with respect. We do not tolerate harassment, bullying, hate speech, or abuse of any kind. Let's build a positive environment.

2. Keep It Safe

Do not share harmful, explicit, or illegal content. We are committed to maintaining a platform where users of all ages can feel secure.
