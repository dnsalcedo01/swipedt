Community Guidelines

Our vision is to build a safe, inclusive, and vibrant social network. Please read and adhere to these guidelines to ensure a positive environment for all members.

Strictly Prohibited

Explicit Material

The distribution of explicit, adult, or non-consensual media is strictly forbidden. Users must maintain a safe environment and respect the privacy of others at all times.

Hate Speech & Discrimination

Hate speech, slurs, and discriminatory language in any form are strictly prohibited. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for abusive language directed at anyone, regardless of background, identity, or beliefs.

Spam & Scams

The dissemination of malicious links, unauthorized promotions, gambling advertisements, or any form of deceptive scams is forbidden. Protect the integrity of the platform.

Misinformation

The deliberate spread of false information, unverified claims, or misleading news designed to deceive the community is prohibited. Users are expected to verify sources before sharing.

Targeted Harassment

Engaging in systematic bullying, targeted harassment, or any organized effort to intimidate and threaten individuals or groups will result in immediate suspension.

Impersonation

The creation of fraudulent accounts to impersonate public figures or other users is prohibited. Parody or fan accounts are permitted strictly on the condition that their unofficial nature is clearly stated.

Disruptive Behavior

Engaging in excessive, repetitive posting or mass-commenting intended to disrupt platform operations, degrade user experience, or intentionally cause server instability is strictly forbidden.

Civic & Political Integrity

To maintain a positive environment, the platform may not be used for political campaigning, lobbying, or aggressive partisan debates. Spreading electoral misinformation, inciting political extremism, or coordinating bot networks to push political agendas is strictly prohibited.

Encouraged Interactions

Respectful Dialogue

Maintain professionalism and courtesy during interactions. Constructive debates are encouraged, provided they remain respectful.

Authenticity & Verification

Foster a culture of trust by sharing authentic experiences and relying on verified, credible sources when disseminating information.

Support & Inclusion

Contribute to a diverse and inclusive community by engaging positively and supporting fellow users across all backgrounds.

See a violation?

Help us maintain a secure platform by actively reporting posts or comments that breach these established guidelines.

Experiencing a bug or need account recovery? Visit our Help Center.
